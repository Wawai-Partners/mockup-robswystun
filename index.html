A note from the person on the other side of the email.

I've been writing for businesses and authors since 2005, and ghostwriting full-length books since 2013. I work alone. No agency overhead, no junior account handler in between, no pivoting to a chatbot mid-project. Every word that leaves this desk is written by one human, on purpose, for you.

The work splits cleanly into two practices: books, where I write full-length manuscripts in your voice; and B2B content, where I write the articles, white papers, case studies, and landing pages your team would rather not roll the dice on.

What both share is careful listening, real research, and prose that sounds like the person it came from. The promise, in one line: 100% human-created, every word, no exceptions. The longer version of that promise lives in section 04.

A standing→policy

Every word on this site, and in your project, composed by a human.

No AI drafting. No AI editing. No AI “polishing.” No machine summary of your interviews, no generated outline, no model-assisted anything.

A model can produce fluent sentences. It cannot produce your sentences — the way you think, the things you'd never say, the joke that only lands because it is actually yours. That is the work. I do it by hand because there is no other way to do it.
